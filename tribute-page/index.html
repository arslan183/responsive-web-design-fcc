Virat Kohli

India's finest Test Captain and World's best Batsman across all formats.

Just after sending a batter back, Kohli glances at the crowd with a silent 'shh' , the fire in his eyes doing all the talking.

Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, is one of the finest cricketers of the modern era. Known for his fiery attitude, unmatched consistency, and extraordinary fitness, he has redefined what it means to be a modern athlete. Kohli has captained India across all formats and has led the team to historic wins overseas. With over 80 international centuries, he is among the highest run-scorers in cricket history. His passion, work ethic, and hunger for victory have inspired millions across the globe.
